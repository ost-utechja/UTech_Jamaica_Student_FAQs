# Accommodation – Frequently Asked Questions

This page provides information about on‑campus and related accommodation options.<br>
Click a question to view the full answer.

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Do students have to live on campus?</strong></summary>

No.<br><br>

Living on campus is optional. The decision to reside on campus is entirely up to the student.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Are there dormitories on campus?</strong></summary>

Yes.<br><br>

The University provides a limited number of residential facilities for full‑time students.<br><br>

There are:
- Seven halls of residence<br>
- Separate accommodation facilities for males and females<br>
- A limited number of spaces, subject to availability<br><br>

Students who are interested in on‑campus accommodation must complete a Boarding Application and submit it to the Student Services Department.<br><br>

For assistance, contact the Accommodation Office:<br>
Email: boarding4utech.edu.jm<br>
Extension: 2841<br>
Office hours: Monday to Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

</details>
