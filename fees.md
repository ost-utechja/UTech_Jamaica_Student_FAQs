## 💰 Enrolment Commitment Deposit (ECD)

<details>
<summary><strong>What is the Enrolment Commitment Deposit (ECD)?</strong></summary>

The Enrolment Commitment Deposit (ECD) is a **mandatory, non‑refundable, non‑transferable** deposit towards each semester’s tuition.

All undergraduate and postgraduate students must pay the ECD **before module selection**.

**ECD amounts:**
- Local students: **JMD $40,000**
- International students: **USD $250**

</details>

## 💳 Tuition & Registration

<details>
<summary><strong>How would I know my tuition fees?</strong></summary>

Use the **Student Tuition Calculator** available on the University’s website.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What are the requirements to get registered?</strong></summary>

To complete registration, students must:

1. **Pay the ECD** (approved payment agencies only)
2. Log in to the **Student Portal**
3. Select and confirm modules (returning students)
4. View fee breakdown
5. Choose a payment plan
6. Accept payment terms and conditions
7. Accept terms and conditions of studentship
8. Pay tuition according to the selected payment plan

Financial clearance is issued **after payment updates**, which take **2 clear business days** (Monday–Friday).

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Are books included in tuition fees?</strong></summary>

No. **Books are not included** in tuition fees.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Do I have to pay my tuition in full at the start of the semester?</strong></summary>

No. Tuition may be paid according to the **payment plan** issued each semester via the student portal.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Will I receive financial clearance if I have not paid at least 50% of my tuition?</strong></summary>

No. Students must pay **at least 50% of tuition** to receive financial clearance.

Failure to do so may prevent access to:
- Classes
- Registration
- University services

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I sit final exams after paying only 50% of tuition?</strong></summary>

No. **Full tuition payment is required each semester** in order to sit final examinations.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What happens if I cannot pay the remaining 50% before final exams?</strong></summary>

You will:
- Not be allowed to sit final exams
- Lose access to exam timetables
- Have the modules removed
- Still owe 50% of tuition for the semester

Students facing financial difficulty should contact:  
📧 fincustomersupport@utech.edu.jm
