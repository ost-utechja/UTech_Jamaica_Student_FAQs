<details>
<summary><strong>How do I take a break from my studies?</strong></summary>

To take a break (leave of absence or withdrawal):

1. Complete a **CSAAC/FSAAC Form**
2. Submit the form to your College/Faculty or the Student Relations Office, Student Services Building

📌 Approval is normally granted for **one (1) year**, which is excluded from the time allowed to complete your programme.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What is the procedure for re-entering the University after a period of absence?</strong></summary>

To re-enter the University:

1. Complete an **Application for Readmission** form  
2. Submit the form to the **Admissions Office**

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>When do students register at the University?</strong></summary>

Registration takes place **three (3) times per year**:
- Semester 1: July–August  
- Semester 2: January  
- Summer Session  

</details>
